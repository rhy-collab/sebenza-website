Programs

Working lockstep with community and government.

Sebenza doesn't just advise from the outside. We build the governance, funding and reporting foundations that let community-led programs run — and prove their impact to the government agencies that fund them.

Who is IRAAC?

IRAAC is an Aboriginal Community Controlled Organisation that Sebenza Advisory partners with closely — supporting its governance, administration, reporting and program delivery so it can meet the expectations of Local Decision Making (LDM), Aboriginal Affairs NSW, ORIC and its funders, while growing the programs it runs for community.

Programs we support

Each program below is delivered by IRAAC and backed by the governance, funding and reporting systems Sebenza builds alongside it.

Governance capability

MCC — Mob and Country Connections

A governance and administrative capability program built on peer support — one Aboriginal Community Organisation helping another, at that organisation's own invitation. MCC strengthens governance, reporting, financial management and leadership confidence, and doubles as a practical readiness assessment for Local Decision Making. It's designed to add value no matter which governance direction an organisation ultimately takes.

How Sebenza supports every program

Behind each program sits the same foundation, so growth in one area strengthens the whole organisation.

01

Funding & proposal development

Program and funding proposals written to a standard government agencies and funders recognise and trust.

02

Governance & Board reporting

Consistent Board reporting frameworks and governance training so every program can demonstrate it is well governed.

03

Digital office & systems

04
