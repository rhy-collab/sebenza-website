What we help with

Sebenza Advisory works alongside Aboriginal Community Organisations, Alliances and Assemblies — and the government agencies that fund them — to build governance, reporting and administrative capability that lasts beyond any single grant or program.

Strategy

Strategic advisory & growth

Planning support, government engagement and organisational growth advice as your organisation takes on more responsibility and larger programs.

Governance

Governance & compliance

Board reporting frameworks, governance training, policy and procedures that meet ORIC, ACNC, Aboriginal Affairs NSW and funder requirements.

Funding

Funding & program development

Program design, funding proposals and reporting support that helps you secure, acquit and grow funded programs with confidence.

Digital

Digital capability

Practical digital office setup and training — email, records, reporting tools and simple systems your whole team can actually use.

Training

On-site capability building

Hands-on training in financial management, reporting, funding applications and governance, built around the real work your team is already doing.

Support

Ongoing administration support

Practical, on-the-ground support for community organisations adopting new systems — remote or on site, until your team is confident running it alone.

Governance structure matters less than governance capability. A well-supported organisation performs well under any funding model — that's where we focus first.

We build systems, templates and training that your own people can run — not dependence on outside consultants.
